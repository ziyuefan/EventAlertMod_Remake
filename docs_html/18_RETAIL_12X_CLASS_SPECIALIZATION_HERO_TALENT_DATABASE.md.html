EventAlertMod 12.x 午夜職業專精與英雄天賦極限實戰資料庫(12.0.7)

本文件詳細記載了EventAlertMod（EAM）在魔獸世界服正式12.0.7版本中，所支援的13個職業、39個專精，以及28個英雄天賦的核心監控配置與實戰校對指標。此資料作為AddOn本地化選項篩選、一鍵載入預設值以及未來AI代理維護裝備庫時的權威參考資料。

🛠️ 監控配置欄位屬性解釋

在庫配置中，除了常用的id（武器ID）與type（武器監控類型，如aura、spellCooldown）之外，我們也為實戰優化配置了下列進階屬性：

  • unit：監控的單位目標，通常為"player"（玩家本身）或"target"（當前目標），寵物為"pet"。
  • pandemicTime: Pandemic Glow（未知亮框）** 效果提示重新整理。
  • maxStacksAlert：溢位防呆層數。當有層數達到此值時，會以高亮或特殊視窗提醒玩家佔用資源，以防止浪費。
  • minStacksAlert：最低警報層數。當發生層數達到此值時才開始警報（或註意提醒）。
  • showValue：數值監控。主要用於防護吸收量（如戰士無視苦痛、武僧天神酒），會在圖示上動態數值。
  • glowOnApply：施放時發光。當光環應用時立即啟用高亮發光。
  • grayOnMove：移動置灰。當感知到玩家移動且無移動施法Buff時，圖示會降低排水度（Alpha = 0.3），提示無法施放。
  • moveBuffs: 移動施法 Buff 清單。只要玩家身上有此列表中的 Buff（如冰浮、懸空、靈行者之賜），即使在移動中，圖標也不置灰。
  • burstFilterActive：爆發警報標記。當特定的爆發Buff（如燃燒）啟動時，自動點亮此傳染病的流行病或提示低優先級警報，減少視線幹擾。

EventAlertMod 12.x Midnight professional specialization and hero talent extreme practical database (12.0.7)

This document records in detail the core monitoring configuration and actual combat calibration indicators of 13 professions, 39 specializations, and 28 hero talents supported by EventAlertMod (EAM) in the official version 12.0.7 of the World of Warcraft server. This information serves as an authoritative reference for AddOn localization option filtering, one-click loading of default values, and future AI agent maintenance of equipment libraries.

🛠️ Explanation of monitoring configuration field attributes

In the library configuration, in addition to the commonly used id (weapon ID) and type (weapon monitoring type, such as Aura, spellCooldown), we have also configured the following advanced attributes for actual combat optimization:

  • unit: The monitored unit target, usually "player" (the player itself) or "target" (the current target), and the pet is "pet".
  • pandemicTime: Pandemic Glow (unknown bright frame)** Effect prompts have been rearranged.
  • maxStacksAlert: Number of overflow foolproof layers. When the number of layers reaches this value, a highlight or special window will be used to remind the player to occupy resources to prevent waste.
  • minStacksAlert: Minimum alarm level. The alarm (or attention reminder) will start when the number of occurrence levels reaches this value.
  • showValue: Numerical monitoring. It is mainly used to protect the absorption amount (such as the warrior ignores pain, the monk's wine of god), and the dynamic value will be displayed on the icon.
  • glowOnApply: Glows when cast. Enables highlight glow immediately when the halo is applied.
  • grayOnMove: Mobile is grayed out. When the player is detected to be moving and there is no movement casting buff, the icon will reduce the drainage level (Alpha = 0.3), indicating that the spell cannot be cast.
  • moveBuffs: List of mobile casting buffs. As long as the player has the Buffs in this list (such as Ice Float, Suspension, Spirit Walker's Gift), the icon will not be grayed out even while moving.
  • burstFilterActive: Outbreak alert flag. When a specific outbreak buff (such as burning) is activated, the epidemic of this infectious disease will be automatically lit or a low-priority alarm will be prompted to reduce line of sight interference.

📌 30% Pandemic Critical Seconds Accurate Proofreading

In the official World of Warcraft server, DoT/HoT refresh follows the Pandemic (lossless inheritance) rules, that is, when the remaining time is less than "30% of the original base duration", it is refreshed, and the remaining time can be used in the next round without loss.

⚡ Resource overflow prevention and high-energy prevention solution strategy (resource safety margin)

In combat, overflow of core resources must be prevented. The definition of EAM is configured with "critical layers" and "anti-fixation threshold", giving players a strong glowing highlight prompt when resources are about to overflow, and prioritizing consumption.

  1. Mage: Arcane - Void Precision (382445)
  2. Mechanism: Maximum 2 levels. When the number of layers = 2, the next arcane impact/barrage will disappear.
  3. Foolproof: maxStacksAlert = 2. When the number of layers reaches 2, the icon will glow strongly to guide the consumption, preventing subsequent layers of Archmage's Touch from being wasted due to missile casting.
  4. Mage: Skysplitter - Magic Shard (Splinter, 444621)
  5. Mechanism: Automatically released up to level 8.
  6. Foolproof: maxStacksAlert = 8.
  7. Shaman: Enhancement - Maelstrom Weapon (344179)
  8. Mechanism: The talent can be upgraded to 10 levels, but if it is not released at 10 levels, subsequent melee white words and storm strikes will cause the clue vortex to fail.
  9. Foolproof: maxStacksAlert = 10. When reaching the 10th floor, the vortex weapon icon on the headset turns to a super glowing state, forcing the player to intersperse instant lightning bolts/elemental shocks.
  10. Warrior: Weapons/Protection - Colossus Might (434947)
  11. Mechanism: Maximum 10 layers, providing a large amount of skill damage gain.
  12. Foolproof: maxStacksAlert = 10, minStacksAlert = 3. It is inconspicuous when there are less than 3 layers, but when it reaches 10 layers, it will start to explode and highlight, prompting the player to enter a violent killing or actively venting force output.
  13. Warlock: Demonology - Demon Core (Demon Core, 264173)
  14. Mechanism: Up to 4 layers can be stored. If it reaches level 4 without consuming it, the core generated when the imp dies or the tyrant disappears will malfunction and be lost.
  15. Foolproof: maxStacksAlert = 4. Forced to highlight when 4 layers are reached, prompting immediate casting of Demonic Arrow.
  16. Death Knight: Blood - Bone Shield (Bone Shield, 195181)
  17. Mechanism: Anti-tank core. It needs to be maintained above 5 layers to enjoy the full damage reduction and bone shield gain.
  18. Modify foolproofing: minStacksAlert = 5 (actual in 12.0.7, originally 3). When the number of layers of the player's bone shield is lower than 5, the icon will trigger a highlighted head (foolproof warning), prompting the player to cast the Host Split Guidance.

🏃 Move and move to get dynamic guidance of skills (dynamic projection and gray display)

In the highly dynamic Great Rift and raid battles of 12.0.7, casting decisions during movement are the watershed that distinguishes top ranged/healing players. The design of EAM implements "action dynamic guidance": When there is a player moving state (GetUnitSpeed("player") > 0), and there is no movement casting buff on him (such as the mage "Ice Floe", the shaman "Spirit Walker's Gift", the evocation master "Hanging"): * Reading Skill: Automatically grayed out (Alpha = 0.3), indicating that it cannot be cast at the moment to avoid interruption of player reading. * Instant skill/Trigger skill: Maintain 100% brightness or highlight to guide players to keep GCD running when moving. * Once the player obtains the Movement Casting Buff, or stops moving: All skills immediately return to normal.
