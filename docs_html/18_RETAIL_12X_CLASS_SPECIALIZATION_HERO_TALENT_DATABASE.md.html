EventAlertMod 12.x Midnight 職業專精與英雄天賦極限實戰資料庫 (12.0.7)

本文件詳細記載了 EventAlertMod（EAM）在魔獸世界正式服 12.0.7 版本中，所支援的 13 個職業、39 個專精，以及 28 個英雄天賦 的核心預設監控配置與實戰校對指標。此資料作為 AddOn 本地化選單篩選、一鍵載入預設值以及未來 AI 代理維護法術庫時的權威參考資料。

🛠️ 監控配置欄位屬性解釋

在法術庫配置中，除了常規的 id（法術 ID）與 type（監控類型，如 aura、spellCooldown）外，我們還為實戰優化配置了以下進階屬性：

  • unit: 監控的單位目標，通常為 "player"（玩家自身）或 "target"（當前目標），寵物為 "pet"。
  • pandemicTime: Pandemic 臨界重新整理時間（秒）。當光傳染/光環剩餘時間低於此值時，Renderer 會自動啟用 Pandemic Glow（邊框亮框閃爍） 效果提示重新整理。
  • maxStacksAlert: 溢出防呆層數。當堆疊層數達到此值時，會以高亮或特殊視覺提醒玩家消耗資源，防範溢出浪費。
  • minStacksAlert: 最低警告層數。當堆疊層數高於此值時才開始警報（或提醒注意）。
  • showValue: 數值量化監控。主要用於護盾吸收量（如戰士無視苦痛、武僧天神酒），會在圖示上動態繪製數值。
  • glowOnApply: 施放時發光。當光環套用時立即啟用高亮發光。
  • grayOnMove: 移動置灰。當檢測到玩家移動且無移動施法 Buff 時，圖示降低飽和度（Alpha = 0.3），提示無法施放。
  • moveBuffs: 移動施法 Buff 列表。只要玩家身上有此列表中的 Buff（如浮冰、懸空、靈行者之賜），即使在移動中，圖示亦不置灰。
  • burstFilterActive: 爆發降噪標記。當特定爆發 Buff（如燃燒）激活時，自動屏蔽此法術的 Pandemic 提示或低優先級警告，減少視覺干擾。

⚡ 資源防溢出與高能防呆判定 (Resource Safety Margins)

在高速戰鬥中，必須預防核心資源的溢出。EAM 的配置定義了「臨界層數」與「防呆閾值」，在資源即將溢出時給予玩家強烈的發光高亮提示，優先消耗。

  1. 法師：秘法 - 虛空精準 (Void Precision, 382445)
  2. 機制：最大 2 層。當層數 = 2 時，下一個秘法衝擊/彈幕必消。
  3. 防呆：maxStacksAlert = 2。當層數達到 2 時，圖示強烈發光，引導消耗，防止後續大法師之觸期間因飛彈施放造成層數浪費。
  4. 法師：裂空者 - 法術碎屑 (Splinter, 444621)
  5. 機制：疊加至 8 層自動釋放。
  6. 防呆：maxStacksAlert = 8。
  7. 薩滿：增強 - 漩渦武器 (Maelstrom Weapon, 344179)
  8. 機制：天賦可疊加至 10 層，但滿 10 層若不釋放，後續近戰白字與風暴打擊會造成漩渦溢出。
  9. 防呆：maxStacksAlert = 10。在達到 10 層時，漩渦武器圖示轉為特大發光狀態，強制玩家穿插瞬發閃電箭/元素衝擊。
  10. 戰士：武器/防護 - 巨像之力 (Colossus Might, 434947)
  11. 機制：最大 10 層，提供大量技能傷害增益。
  12. 防呆：maxStacksAlert = 10, minStacksAlert = 3。當小於 3 層時不顯眼，但疊滿 10 層時啟動爆發高亮，提示玩家進入狂暴斬殺或主動洩力輸出。
  13. 術士：惡魔學識 - 惡魔核心 (Demonic Core, 264173)
  14. 機制：最大可儲存 4 層。若達到 4 層而不消耗，小鬼死亡或暴君消失時產生的核心將會溢出丟失。
  15. 防呆：maxStacksAlert = 4。在滿 4 層時強制高亮，提示立即施放瞬發惡魔之箭。
  16. 死亡騎士：鮮血 - 骸骨之盾 (Bone Shield, 195181)
  17. 機制：防戰坦核心。需要維持在 5 層以上才能享受完整減傷與骨盾增益。
  18. 防呆：minStacksAlert = 5 (12.0.7 實戰修正，原為 3)。當玩家骨盾層數低於 5 層時，圖示觸發黃色高亮閃爍（防呆警告），督促玩家施放骨髓分裂。

🔇 爆發超載降噪與視覺優先級 (Burst Period De-noising)

在爆發期，玩家的 GCD 極度緊湊。此時，EAM 應當進入「爆發戰術模式」，屏蔽無關的常規冷卻、防禦護盾等「雜訊圖示」，而應將全部視覺資源集中在核心輸出觸發與短 CD 爆發技上。

🏃 移動走位可用技能動態引導 (Dynamic Casting & Gray-out)

在 12.0.7 的高動態大秘境與團本戰鬥中，走位期間的施法判定是區分頂尖遠程/治療玩家的分水嶺。EAM 的設計實作了「移動動態引導」： 當玩家處於移動狀態（GetUnitSpeed("player") > 0），且身上沒有移動施法 Buff（如法師「浮冰」、薩滿「靈行者之賜」、喚能師「懸空」）時： * 讀條技能：自動置灰（Alpha = 0.3），提示目前無法施放，避免玩家原地讀條中斷。 * 瞬發技能 / 觸發技能：保持 100% 亮度或高亮，指引玩家在走位時維持 GCD 運轉。 * 一旦玩家獲得移動施法 Buff，或停止移動：所有技能立即恢復正常。

EventAlertMod 12.x Midnight Professional Specialization and Hero Talent Extreme Practical Database (12.0.7)

This document details the core default monitoring configuration and actual combat calibration indicators of the 13 professions, 39 specialties, and 28 hero talents supported by EventAlertMod (EAM) in the official World of Warcraft server version 12.0.7. This information serves as the authoritative reference for AddOn localization menu filtering, one-click loading of defaults, and future AI agents when maintaining spell libraries.

🛠️ Explanation of monitoring configuration field attributes

In the spell library configuration, in addition to the conventional id (spell ID) and type (monitoring type, such as aura, spellCooldown), we have also configured the following advanced attributes for actual combat optimization:

  • unit: The monitored unit target, usually "player" (player itself) or "target" (current target), and the pet is "pet".
  • pandemicTime: Pandemic critical refresh time (seconds). When the remaining time of the light contagion/halo is lower than this value, the Renderer will automatically enable the Pandemic Glow (border bright frame flashing) effect prompt to rearrange.
  • maxStacksAlert: Overflow foolproof stacks. When the number of stacking layers reaches this value, a highlight or special visual will be used to remind the player to consume resources to prevent overflow and waste.
  • minStacksAlert: The minimum number of warning layers. The alarm (or reminder) will only start when the number of stacked layers is higher than this value.
  • showValue: Numerical quantitative monitoring. It is mainly used for shield absorption (such as warriors ignoring pain, monks deity wine), and the value will be dynamically drawn on the icon.
  • glowOnApply: Glow when applied. Enables highlight glow immediately when the halo is applied.
  • grayOnMove: Move gray. When player movement is detected and there is no movement casting buff, the icon is desaturated (Alpha = 0.3), indicating that it cannot be cast.
  • moveBuffs: List of move casting buffs. As long as the player has the Buffs in this list (such as Ice Floe, Suspension, Spirit Walker's Gift), the icon will not be grayed out even while moving.
  • burstFilterActive: Burst noise reduction flag. Automatically mutes this spell's Pandemic prompt or low-priority warning when a specific burst buff (such as Burn) is active, reducing visual distractions.

📌 30% Pandemic Critical Seconds Accurate Proofreading

In the official World of Warcraft server, DoT/HoT refresh follows the Pandemic (lossless inheritance) rule, that is, it is refreshed when the remaining time is less than "30% of the original base duration", and the remaining time can be superimposed to the next round without loss.

⚡ Resource overflow prevention and high-energy foolproof judgment (Resource Safety Margins)

In high-speed battles, overflow of core resources must be prevented. The configuration of EAM defines "critical layers" and "fool-proof threshold", giving players a strong glowing highlight prompt when resources are about to overflow, and prioritize consumption.

  1. Mage: Arcane - Void Precision (382445)
  2. Mechanism: Max 2 levels. When the number of layers = 2, the next Arcane Blast/Barrage must disappear.
  3. Foolproof: maxStacksAlert = 2. When the number of layers reaches 2, the icon will glow strongly to guide the consumption, preventing the wastage of layers due to missile casting during the subsequent Archmage's Touch.
  4. Mage: Skysplitter - Spell Shard (Splinter, 444621)
  5. Mechanism: Automatically released when stacked to 8 layers.
  6. Foolproof: maxStacksAlert = 8.
  7. Shaman: Enhancement - Maelstrom Weapon (344179)
  8. Mechanism: The talent can be stacked up to 10 layers, but if it is not released after 10 layers, subsequent melee white letters and storm strikes will cause the vortex to overflow.
  9. Foolproof: maxStacksAlert = 10. Upon reaching level 10, the Vortex weapon icon changes to a super glowing state, forcing the player to intersperse instant Lightning Bolt/Elemental Blast.
  10. Warrior: Weapons/Protection - Colossus Might (434947)
  11. Mechanism: Maximum 10 stacks, providing massive skill damage buffs.
  12. Foolproof: maxStacksAlert = 10, minStacksAlert = 3. It is inconspicuous when there are less than 3 layers, but when it reaches 10 layers, it will start to explode and highlight, prompting the player to enter a violent killing or actively vent force output.
  13. Warlock: Demonology - Demonic Core (264173)
  14. Mechanism: Up to 4 levels can be stored. If it reaches level 4 without consuming it, the cores generated when the imp dies or the tyrant disappears will be overflowed and lost.
  15. Foolproof: maxStacksAlert = 4. Forced highlight at 4 stacks, prompting instant Demonic Arrow to be cast.
  16. Death Knight: Blood - Bone Shield (Bone Shield, 195181)
  17. Mechanism: Anti-tank core. It needs to be maintained above 5 layers to enjoy the full damage reduction and bone shield gain.
  18. Foolproof: minStacksAlert = 5 (12.0.7 combat fix, originally 3). When the number of layers of the player's bone shield is less than 5, the icon triggers a yellow highlighted flash (foolproof warning), urging the player to cast Marrow Splitting.

🔇Burst Period De-noising and Visual Priority (Burst Period De-noising)

During a burst, a player's GCD is extremely tight. At this time, EAM should enter the "burst tactical mode", block irrelevant "noise icons" such as regular cooling and defensive shields, and focus all visual resources on core output triggers and short CD burst skills.

Noise reduction and highlight filtering rules (Burst Filtering)

  • Mage (Fire - Combustion 190319 Period):
  • Noise Reduction: Hides the Pandemic critical flicker of Living Bomb (117828). Because the burning period is all instantaneous Pyroblast and Fire Blast, manually replenishing bombs is a serious waste of GCD and DPS loss.

🏃 Dynamic casting skills can be used to guide movement (Dynamic Casting & Gray-out)

In the highly dynamic Greater Rift and raid battles of 12.0.7, cast decisions during movement are what separate the top ranged/healing players. The design of EAM implements "mobile dynamic guidance": When the player is in moving state (GetUnitSpeed("player") > 0) and does not have a movement casting buff on him (such as the mage "Ice Floe", the shaman "Spirit Walker's Gift", the summoner "Hanging"): * Reading skill: automatically grayed out (Alpha = 0.3), indicating that it cannot be cast at the moment to avoid interruption of player reading. * Instant skill/Trigger skill: Maintain 100% brightness or highlight to guide players to maintain GCD operation when moving. * Once the player gains the Movement Casting Buff, or stops moving: All skills immediately return to normal.
